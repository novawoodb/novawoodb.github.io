What is Green Doctor AI?

Green Doctor AI is an Android mobile application that uses artificial intelligence to diagnose plant diseases, lawn problems, and provide treatment recommendations. Simply take a photo of your plant or lawn and get instant AI-powered diagnosis.

Features

Plant Health Scan - Identify diseases, pests, and nutrient deficiencies.

Grass and Lawn Scan - Multi-photo lawn analysis with treatment plans.

Flower Scan - Flower identification and care guide.

Garden and Vegetable Grow Guide - Step by step growing instructions.

Weather Protection - Seasonal garden protection plans.

Amazon Product Recommendations - Find treatment products instantly.
